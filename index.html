Game Creation

My top down shooter game was a thought that I have being thinking of for a game design idea, that I thought would be appealing to age range of teenage boys and some girls that enjoy the aspect of these sorts of games. About Me

My name is Fabiana Lombardi, I am 19 years old and I am currently studying at Harrogate College after graduating from Ripon Grammar School after finishing my Lower Sixth Form Year. At the moment I am studying a BTEC Level 3 in IT nd hope to continue on to University to start a degree reading Computer Science. While at Ripon Grammar School, I completed my GCSEs in Maths, English, Science and French as well as many more (9 in total), all ranging from A-C. I also completed an AS Level in Maths.
In fourth year of my high school education, I undertook work experience with BackUp Technologies who provide technical support for computer systems all around the world including some major companies such as BBC. This is where I wrote my first digital blog, which was inserted into their newsletter, about the introduction and development of Google Glasses. This type of experience in the IT industry has only furthered my decision to study computer science. It has also increased my knowledge of different types of computer systems such as Linux and Mac OS as well as coding software’s.

Most of my outer college learning for this subject has come from the internet through the website FutureLearn gaining qualifications in Cyber Security, Digital Marketing, Web Science, Data to Insight and also the Basics in Java Programming. When participating in the programming course I encountered the basic instructions to create a simplistic mobile game, while also gathering certificates to signify that I had completed the courses to the required specifications. I have found these highly stimulating and enriching to my education, giving me a more valuable experience enabling me to get on the path in which I would like to follow.

My love of problem solving has become a major factor with my work at Decon Solutions as I am undertaking situations I have never encountered before and after completing each problem, it gives me an increased sense of enlightenment after realising that every problem has a solution. This also leaves me with a great anticipation for the next problem that needs to be solved. I have also embarked on producing an easy to use financial worksheet in order to calculate their VAT for each expenditure and transaction made, therefore resulting in a simple and time efficient format.

My class was elected to represent Harrogate College for an anti-cyber bullying campaign which involved producing a video to be shown on YouTube, which later won an award for the best creativity out of the entire Hull group of colleges. This helped develop my teamwork and responsibilities to help shed light on an overlooked sensitive topic.

My interests span further than that of computers. I am a keen boxer and enjoy participating in a local boxing group. I first decided that I was interested in boxing when I wanted to find a fun way in which I could increase my fitness and be able to unwind from the stress of college and school. Overall I have managed to increase my agility and coordination by taking part in different activities and sparring with others in a controlled environment showing that I am competitive and focused.

What excites me the most about computer science is the fact that we have only just begun to scratch the surface of their limitless capabilities. I am looking forward to becoming part of the next female generation of computer scientists to bring forward new attributes and build on the work of today’s greatest minds.
